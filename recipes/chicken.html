Creamy chicken breast

Description

I LOVE this creamy chicken. Seriously.

Serve with lots of cheese. And pasta. Anything you want.

Ingredients

  • Chicken breasts
  • Cooking cream
  • Spices
  • Olive oil
  • Any cheese!

Steps

  1. Prepare the chicken breasts with the spices
  2. Heat pan, add olive oil
  3. Place chicken breats in oven
  4. Prepare chicken breats and then add the cooking cream
  5. Serve with rice, pasta, anything!!! Enjoy!!!
