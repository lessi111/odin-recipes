Pancakes

Description

The simplest flour recipe: pancakes. Easy to make, just about everyone can do it.

It is orderless - just throw all ingredients and mix.

Ingredients

  • 200g flour
  • 300g milk
  • 40g melted butter
  • 30g sugar
  • 2g salt
  • 2g baking powder
  • 2 eggs

Steps

  1. Whisk the eggs in a bowl, and add the milk without stopping
  2. Add the rest of ingredients and keep whisking until you get an uniform batter
  3. Rest the batter for 15min
  4. Heat a frying pan, and oil it with butter (do this only for the first pancake)
  5. When hot, place one pancake-worth of batter in the pan
  6. Wait a minute, then flip it. Then wait another minute, and retrieve
  7. Repeat!
