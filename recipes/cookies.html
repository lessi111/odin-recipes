Cookies

Description

Want some cookies? Bring 5 ingredients (or less)!

Serve with a cup of tea.

Ingredients

  • 275g flour
  • 225g butter, softened
  • 110g caster sugar
  • 1 tsp cinnamon or other spices (optional)
  • 75g white or milk chocolate chips (optional)

Steps

  1. Heat oven to 170c-190c
  2. Cream butter in a large bowl, until soft
  3. Add sugar and beat until the mixture is light
  4. Sift flour and add optional ingredients
  5. Bring mixture together in a figure-of-eight motion, until dough
  6. Freeze the dough! (optional)
  7. Roll dough into walnut-sized balls and place them slightly apart in a baking sheet
  8. Flatten balls a little with the palm of your hand
  9. Bake them for around 10-12 minutes, until golden brown and slightly firm on top
  10. Leave cookies on a cooling rack for around 15 minutes before serving
  11. Enjoy!
